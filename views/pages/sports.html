SPORTS AND EVENT

The Badminton Competition

The badminton competition was a huge success, with a diverse group of talented athletes showcasing their talents. The participants were evaluated on technique, strategy, and overall performance. The competition provided for young sportspersons to show their skills and gain valuable experience in the competitive sports world. The event was well organized, and the audience thoroughly enjoyed the performances. Overall, it was a memorable experience for everyone involved.

Box Cricket Competition

The time taken for a match was approximately 15-20 minutes. The competition was well-attended, with 15 teams from the college and 2 teams from the Architecture college who showcased their talents. The event was lapsed to the Semifinals and Finals. Since there were an odd number of teams, a team got directly to the finals. There were 12 matches conducted to decide the winner. There was also a team for maintaining the discipline of the audience who managed the crowd quite well.

Rink Football Competition

The Rink football competition is on 02nd February 2023. Each team had 3 members each. The maximum number of group members allowed was 3. The time limit to be maintained is 3 minutes per half. The competition was well-attended, with 10 participants from the college who showcased their talents. The event was a clear knockout stage. There were 10 matches conducted to decide the winner. There was also a team for maintaining the discipline of the audience who managed the crowd quite well.

Volleyball Competition

The Volleyball competition is on 02nd February 2023. Each team had 7 members each. The maximum number of group members allowed was 7. The time limit was 3 sets each. The competition was well-attended, with 3 participants from the college who showcased their talents. The event was a clear knockout stage. There were 6 matches conducted to decide the winner. There was also a team for maintaining the discipline of the audience who managed the crowd quite well. The event was well organized, and the audience thoroughly enjoyed the performances. Overall, it was a memorable experience for everyone involved.

Orientation

Geeky Devolution is the first coding club at LTCE, and its orientation was held on March 10, 2023. The President of the student chapter of CSI-LTCE, Shubham Rana, gave a talk on the importance of coding and how the coding club would help students to develop their coding skills. This report provides an overview of the orientation and the key points discussed by him.

FUNDAMENTALS OF PROGRAMMING

Fundamentals of Programming 11th October, 2022 Geeky Devolution conducted its first session aimed at teaching the fundamentals of programming to junior year students. The session was divided into three parts, where three programming languages were taught, including C++, Python, and Java. The lectures were delivered by final year students. The following report covers the details of the session. Session Overview The session was started with an introduction to programming, followed by a brief history of programming languages.

Peer Mentoring Session

The Coding Club at Lokmanya Tilak College of Engineering organized a peer mentoring session for the first-year students. The objective of the session was to provide guidance and support to the students in learning programming languages such as Java, C++, and Python. The mentoring session was conducted in three different classes, with each class focusing on one programming language.

Basic Of C++ Programming
